Cellphone - Designing a cellphone using Autodesk Alias and VRED

I made a cellphone model for the final assignment of a course in industrial CAD design. I built it by starting simple and adding complexity as I went along. The whole thing was designed in Autodesk Alias using surface modelling.

As soon as the model was finished it was time to make some renders in Autodesk VRED and I am very happy with the end result of this slim looking phone.
